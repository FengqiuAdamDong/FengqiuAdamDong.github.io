About

I am a current 3rd year PhD candidate at the University of British Columbia. My research focusses on using CHIME/FRB and CHIME/Pulsar to find FRBs and pulsars. Futher, I am especially interested in their population. Currently I am the leader for the Galactic (Pulsar) Working Group for CHIME/FRB.
